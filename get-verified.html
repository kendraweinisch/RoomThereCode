Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is checked?

Our Comprehensive Verification Process

To ensure a trusted community, every member undergoes a rigorous screening that confirms their identity and background. The process is tailored for complete coverage:

  • Government ID Validation for all members.
  • Domestic Background Check for residents, including criminal history and the national sex offender registry.
  • Global Background Check for international students, ensuring worldwide safety standards.

This layered approach provides peace of mind and fosters a secure environment for everyone.

How much does it cost?

The fee for the verification is $29.99 and includes RoomThere's processing fee.

Who sees my report?

Your full report is private. RoomThere only receives a confirmation that you have successfully passed the verification, which triggers the "Verified Badge" on your profile. You can access your full report anytime through your Authenticate account.

What if I find an error in my report?

Authenticate has a dedicated, legally-compliant process for disputing inaccuracies. You will be able to manage any disputes directly with Authenticate through their secure portal.

I'm already verified on another platform. Can I use that?

Currently, to ensure consistency and security for all our users, we require the verification to be completed through our integrated partner, Authenticate.

Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

RoomThere collects information that you provide directly to us when you:

  • Create an account
  • Complete your profile
  • Use our messaging system
  • Contact our support team

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Facilitate connections between homeowners and renters
  • Send you technical notices and support messages
  • Respond to your comments and questions

3. Information Sharing

RoomThere does not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share your information in the following limited circumstances:

  • With other users to facilitate matches (profile information only)
  • With service providers who assist our operations
  • When required by law or to protect our rights

4. Background Check Information

If you choose to complete a background check through our third-party partner:

  • Background checks are processed by accredited third-party providers
  • RoomThere does not store sensitive background check data
  • We only receive verification of completion status
  • Full reports are managed directly between you and the background check provider

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Choices

You can:

  • Access and update your profile information
  • Control your communication preferences
  • Delete your account at any time

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at hello@roomthere.com.
